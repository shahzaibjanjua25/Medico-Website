Gynecological Checkup

PREVENT DISEASES WITH REGULAR EXAMINATIONS

To prevent serious diseases such as changes to the cervix or breast, I recommend undergoing regular gynecological checkups at intervals. Even women without any complaints should do this once a year. This is the only way to detect and treat diseases in a timely manner.
